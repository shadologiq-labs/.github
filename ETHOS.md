# What we're about

Something you use every day is a little broken. A clunky screen at work, an app at home that buries the thing you actually want. You've learned to live with it, because fixing it was always someone else's job and they were never going to get to it.

That's the gap we work in. And it's smaller than it used to be, because now you have an AI that can help you do something about it.

ShadoLogiq Labs is a place for exactly that. Three simple things:

- **We find the good stuff.** When something already does the job well, we say so — and we tell you honestly what to watch out for. No "best of" SEO games, no affiliate links. Just an honest list of things worth using. (That's the [catalog](https://github.com/shadologiq-labs/catalog).)
- **We build what's missing.** When the right thing doesn't exist, we make it — a person and their AI, working together. The AI does the heavy lifting; the person brings the taste and the "no, that's not quite right."
- **We help you share it.** This is the part nobody else makes easy. You made something good — now your coworkers and friends want it, and suddenly you're stuck wrestling app stores, accounts, and fees just to hand it over. We take that pain off your plate.

That last one is why we exist at all. Your AI can build you almost anything. What it *can't* do is be the trustworthy name that puts your tool in other people's hands. We can.

## "Why would I trust you with my thing?"

Fair question. Here's the honest answer: there's nothing to worry about, because there's no catch and no one to be the catch.

> "No profile, open, zero risk in us being the source of your tool — we aren't going to steal it; it's already public; we just make it easier."

We don't take ownership. We don't run ads or sell anything. There's no company behind a curtain, no founder building a following, nobody who profits if you do. Your work stays yours, out in the open. We just smooth the last, annoying step. Quietly making things easier is the whole offer — not the start of a pitch.

## Four ways in — pick whichever fits

You don't have to commit to anything. Come in wherever you're comfortable:

1. **Just fix your thing.** Grab a ready-made prompt, paste it into your AI, done. Nothing owed, nobody to ask.
2. **Tell us your idea.** Maybe it becomes something we build.
3. **Build it with us.** Roll up your sleeves on something already in motion.
4. **Start your own.** Take how we do things and run it yourself, your way. Wave if you get stuck.

The fourth one is the real dream — not you sending us requests and waiting, but you doing your own thing, on your own terms, with your own AI. The whole point is that you don't have to wait in anyone's line anymore.

## The one rule

> **Paying for good tools is fine. Settling isn't — not when something better is free.**

We're not against spending money or trusting a company. We're against putting up with something worse when something better is right there, free, and yours to keep. We find it when it exists, build it when it doesn't, and help you pass it along either way.
